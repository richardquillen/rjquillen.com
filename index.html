About

Playing music should be about having fun and that's exactly what I emphasize in my lessons.

Yes, technique, rhythm, lead-playing and tone all take time and patience to develop, but having fun in the process is just as important for making progress.

I cater lessons to each student's goals. If you’re a beginner looking to get started, bring me a few songs you want to learn and we’ll build a curriculum that helps you grow toward playing them confidently.

My own playing is rooted mostly in rock, classic rock, punk, hardcore, post-hardcore, and metal, but I listen to and explore all kinds of music. I'm always happy to dive into the styles that interest my students.

Outside of lessons, I make music, play in local bands, write software and spend time with my wonderful family.
